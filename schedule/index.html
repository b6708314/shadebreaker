Schedule

Pick a time that works.

Three ways to get on the calendar — depending on where you are. Initial conversations are free. I'll read your intake before we meet so we don't spend our first fifteen minutes on throat-clearing.

All times shown in your local timezone. Confirmations come by email immediately.

Discovery Call

Free · 30 min

A short first conversation. You tell me what you're wrestling with; I listen, ask a few sharpening questions, and tell you honestly whether ShadeBreaker is the right fit or whether you'd be better served somewhere else.

Best for: Leaders considering an engagement, peers exploring collaboration, or anyone who's read the essays and wants to talk through the ideas.

Ministry Consultation

Free · 60 min

Longer-form first conversation for church and para-church leaders. We'll talk through your situation — mission drift, leadership transition, structural tension — and I'll give you an honest read on whether a formal engagement would help.

Best for: Pastors, executive pastors, and ministry executives carrying the weight of the next decision. For now, the first ministry conversation is always free.

Strategy Session

Paid · 90 min · coming soon

A focused working session — not a pitch, not a discovery call. You bring a specific decision, question, or plan. We spend 90 minutes pressure-testing it together and you walk out with a sharpened direction.

Best for: Returning clients, leaders who've already had a discovery call, or anyone who needs a single high-leverage working session rather than a longer engagement.

Opening soon. If you already know this is what you need, start with a free discovery call and we'll get you on the schedule directly.

None of these quite fit?

Some conversations don't fit a booking flow — introductions, speaking inquiries, or just something the standard options don't cover. Email insights@shadebreaker.com directly and we'll find a time.
